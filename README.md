# LearnOS: Your Personal Learning Command Center

## Project Overview

LearnOS is an innovative web-based personal learning system designed to empower students and lifelong learners with a centralized command center for managing their educational journey. This application transforms passive learning into an active, organized, and data-driven experience, helping users track progress, manage tasks, and optimize their study habits.

## Key Features

LearnOS offers a comprehensive suite of features tailored to enhance the learning process:

### 1. User Authentication & Cloud Sync

- **Secure Sign-In/Sign-Up:** Seamless account creation and login using email and password.
- **Personalized Experience:** New users can set up their display name, while returning users benefit from cloud-synced data, ensuring continuity across sessions.
- **Owner Login:** Dedicated access for administrators or instructors to manage the system.

### 2. Intuitive Dashboard

- **Overall Progress Tracking:** Visualize your academic journey with a clear, circular progress indicator showing your overall completion percentage.
- **Monthly Attendance:** Monitor your presence and engagement with a dedicated attendance tracker.
- **Subject Progress Overview:** Get a quick glance at your advancement across different subjects.
- **Task Summary:** A consolidated view of all your tasks, categorized by total, completed, pending, and cancelled statuses.
- **Today's Study Time:** Detailed insights into daily study duration, number of sessions, average session length, and weekly study trends, complemented by a visual chart.

### 3. Subject Management

- **Learning Roadmaps:** Organize your studies by creating and managing subjects, each with its own roadmap of topics.
- **Customization:** Assign unique icons and colors to subjects for better organization and visual distinction.
- **Progress Monitoring:** Track the completion status of topics within each subject.

### 4. Task Organization

- **Flexible Task Creation:** Add new tasks with essential details such as name, associated subject, priority level (High, Medium, Low), and deadline.
- **Status Management:** Easily update task statuses to Pending, Completed, or Cancelled.
- **Actionable Tasks:** Edit or delete tasks as your learning priorities evolve.

### 5. Study Timer

- **Focused Study Sessions:** Initiate timed study sessions linked to specific subjects and tasks.
- **Real-time Tracking:** A dynamic timer displays the duration of your active study, with clear indicators for running or paused states.
- **Session History:** Review past study sessions, grouped by date, showing total study time per day and individual session details.

### 6. Attendance Tracking

- **Visual Calendar:** A calendar-like interface to mark and view your attendance, providing a clear overview of your presence.

### 7. Notes & Reminders

- **Versatile Note-Taking:** Create various types of entries including general notes, specific tasks, innovative ideas, and timely reminders.
- **Content Rich:** Each entry supports a title and detailed content, allowing for comprehensive documentation of your thoughts and learning.

### 8. Data Export

- **Backup & Portability:** Export all your personal learning data (subjects, tasks, sessions, attendance, leaves, notes) into a structured JSON file for backup or external analysis.

## Technology Stack

LearnOS is built with a focus on modern web technologies, ensuring a responsive and engaging user experience:

- **Frontend:** HTML5, CSS3 (with custom properties for theming), JavaScript
- **Styling:** Custom CSS with a dark theme, utilizing `Inter` and `Sora` fonts for a clean and professional aesthetic.
- **Backend/Data Storage:** Firebase (implied by script analysis for authentication and data handling).
